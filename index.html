Bisan Subba is a 3D Generalist and Game Developer specializing in creating interactive digital experiences, 3D modeling, and environment design using Unreal Engine (C++/Blueprints), Blender, and Substance Painter. He has developed Android games such as Bsun Obstacle and shares 3D artwork on Pexels.

Key Details

  • Specialization: Game development, 3D modeling, sculpting, animation, and world creation.
  • Tools: Unreal Engine, Blender, Plasticity, Substance Painter, Photoshop and Marmoset Toolbag.
  • Platform Games: Creator of "Bsun Obstacle" available on itch.io and Google Play.
  • Art Portfolio: Shares 3D art publicly online.
